# McDonald's Sales Dashboard (Excel Project)
Project Overview
This project is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze
McDonald's sales performance, profitability, customer metrics, and
customer satisfaction. The dashboard provides a high-level business view
through KPI cards and visualizations, helping stakeholders monitor
performance and identify trends.
---
Dashboard Preview
The dashboard contains:
Sales KPI
Profit KPI
Customer KPI
Customer Satisfaction Analysis
Monthly Sales Trend (2025 vs 2026)
Country-wise Sales Performance
---
Business Objectives
The primary objectives of this dashboard are:
Track sales performance against targets.
Monitor profit achievement.
Measure customer growth.
Analyze customer satisfaction factors.
Compare monthly sales trends.
Identify top-performing countries.

Dashboard Components
1. Sales KPI Doughnut Chart
Purpose: - Displays percentage of sales target achieved. - Highlights
remaining target percentage.
Insights: - Sales reached 85% of target. - Additional effort is required
to achieve full target.
---
2. Profit KPI Doughnut Chart
Purpose: - Tracks profit achievement against business goals.
Insights: - Profit achieved 89% of target. - Indicates strong
profitability performance.
---
3. Customer KPI Doughnut Chart
Purpose: - Measures customer acquisition performance.
Insights: - Customer achievement stands at 87%. - Customer growth
initiatives may improve performance.
---
4. Customer Satisfaction Radar Chart
Customer satisfaction factors analyzed:
Category       Score
---
Speed          54%
Quality        86%
Hygiene        93%
Service        53%
Availability   95%
Key Findings: - Availability is the strongest area. - Hygiene
performance is excellent. - Service and Speed require improvement.
---
5. Sales Trend Analysis (2025 vs 2026)
Purpose: - Compare monthly sales between years. - Identify growth
patterns and seasonality.
Observations: - 2026 outperformed 2025 in most months. - Significant
growth observed during Q4. - August recorded the lowest sales in 2025.
Business Value: - Helps management forecast future demand. - Supports
strategic planning.
